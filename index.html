The Known Universe

Arrakis

Also known as Dune. The only source of the spice melange. A desert wasteland populated by giant sandworms and the Fremen.

The Spice

Melange. It extends life, expands consciousness, and makes interstellar travel possible. The most valuable substance in the universe.

Bene Gesserit

An ancient sisterhood of spies, nuns, scientists, and theologians who use genetic breeding and mental conditioning to guide humanity.
